True End-to-End Encryption

Our foundation rests on Client-Side Cryptography via the Web Crypto API. Your device generates an RSA-2048 key pair upon registration. Messages are encrypted with dynamic AES-256-GCM symmetric keys, which are then encrypted using the recipient's RSA Public Key. The server never sees your private keys or your plaintext. To the server infrastructure, your conversations are indistinguishable from random noise.

Secure Real-Time WebSockets

Powered by Socket.IO over secure channels, instant text delivery is guaranteed. Includes uncompromised support for 1-on-1 exchanges and multi-user group messaging through distributed communal AES keys.

Cryptographic Credit Economy & Moderation

Communicate intelligently. New accounts are outfitted with localized communication credits. Admins rigorously control requests for expanded usage through a dedicated moderation dashboard, maintaining infrastructure health.

Untraceable WebRTC A/V Feeds

SecureConnect leverages WebRTC to establish direct peer-to-peer data channels for high-fidelity audio and video feeds. Packets travel directly between clients, bypassing centralized servers entirely. Instead of a vulnerable centralized mixing server for group calls, we construct a fully distributed Mesh Network where each participant maintains direct, DTLS-SRTP encrypted streams with every other member. Your voice and video metadata never touch our infrastructure.
